Outside the Greenhouse

This is what the exterior of the Greenhouse currently looks like. As it can be seen, the "Greenhouse" is the literal green house fitted with translucent panels that allows light to come in and heat to stay in. The smaller green house on the left is the stroage shed that will hold any tools and equipment that is needed. The solar panel is what is currently powering the Greenhouse.

Inside the Greenhouse

As shown in the above image, the interior of the Greenhouse needs a lot more work to become operational. The interior needs to be cleared out of the miscellaneous objects that are currently being stored, as well as fitted with sensors and actuators in order to collect data about the Greenhouse. The ones tasked with completing this task will be the students of ECE 597, some of which are present in the above image.

Vegetation

There are fourteen enclosures that are currently present just ouside the Greenhouse. Some of these enclosures currently hold some plants, but they are still under construction and will eventually be fitted with a wider variety of plant life. This location is also where the water irrigation system will be implemented, not only to be able to water the plants, but to also make sure there isn't flooding when it rains; as shown above.
